Workshop flow

How this workshop works

Each session moves from concept to simulation to a real build. You will test often, document your decisions, and share what you discover.

Maker mindset: a bug is not failure. It is information that helps you improve the circuit, code, or idea.
  1. Learn the basics
  2. Simulate in Tinkercad
  3. Build safely
  4. Document progress
  5. Share what you made
