Safety rules

  • Disconnect power before changing physical wiring.
  • Never connect a project to a wall outlet.
  • Use only approved workshop power sources.
  • Check LED direction before powering the circuit.
  • Check power and ground connections.
  • Use a resistor with LEDs.
  • Ask for a power check before turning on a new physical circuit.
  • Report damaged, unusually hot, smoking, or burning components immediately.
  • Keep liquids away from electronics.
  • Return components to the correct container.
