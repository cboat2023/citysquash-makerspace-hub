Key vocabulary

Component
A part used in a circuit.
Simulation
A safe digital test of how a circuit should behave.
Ground
The return path that completes the circuit.
Required task

Tinkercad Challenge

Build a circuit containing one power source or Arduino, one LED, one resistor, jumper wires, and a complete path back to ground.

  • Confirm that the LED turns on.
  • Rename your project.
