Before building

Safety and respect checklist

The pledge helps protect people, tools, materials, and student ideas.

Sign the Pledge
  • I will build safely.
  • I will unplug power before changing wires.
  • I will ask before connecting batteries, motors, or unfamiliar parts.
  • I will never connect power directly to ground.
  • I will keep food and water away from electronics.
  • I will respect tools, materials, and other people's projects.
  • I will report heat, smoke, burning smells, broken parts, or unsafe behavior.
  • I will clean my workspace before leaving.
  • I will document my work honestly.
  • I will use projects to learn, help, and create.
