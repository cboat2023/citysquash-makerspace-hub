Your Makerspace Journey

Projects rarely move in a straight line. Move backward or forward between stages whenever testing gives you new information.

  1. 1

    Choose

    Select a project, challenge, or problem.

  2. 2

    Plan

    Draw the idea and identify the components you need.

  3. 3

    Simulate

    Test the circuit or code in Tinkercad when possible.

  4. 4

    Build

    Construct the physical version carefully.

  5. 5

    Test & Debug

    Find problems, make changes, and test again.

  6. 6

    Share

    Document what you learned and present the project.

Remember: Makers move backward and forward as they learn. That is part of the process.
