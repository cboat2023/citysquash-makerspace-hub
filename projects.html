Approval step

What Projects Get Approved?

Before building with physical parts, make sure your idea is safe, useful, and possible.

  • Helps a person, group, classroom, family, or community
  • Safe to build
  • Uses available materials
  • Possible to finish during the workshop
  • Starts with a simple version first
  • Has a Tinkercad simulation before physical building
  • Student can explain how it works
